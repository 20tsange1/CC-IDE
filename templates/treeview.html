This page is designed for the visualisation of contracts in various forms.
Tree: A tree representation of the contract (Parse Tree with respect to CC-Language). It may be hidden at first if the contract is large. Scroll sideways to reach the tree.
Hierarchical: A hierarchical structure of the contract with inclusion of node types, equivalent to the tree but displayed in a textual manner.
AST: A customisable version of the Hierarchical Structure. Using the configuration at the bottom, you can design your own Abstract Syntax Tree.
Boolean: An abstracted version of the contract with Statements and Conditions extracted.
